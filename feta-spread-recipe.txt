Ingredients:

-1/4 lb feta (recommend israeli or bulgarian, but any will do)
-1/4 cup chopped green onions or chives
-salt (to taste)
-oil (to taste
-water crackers or bread

Process
-Crumble feta with fork into clumps
-Add oil, 1 teaspoon at a time, and mix until a paste but still
thick with chunks still mixed in
-Add salt to taste and mix
-Add green onions and mix trying to not smash green onions
-Serve with water crackers or bread
