Ingredients:

-1/4 lb feta (recommend israeli or bulgarian, but any will do)
-1/4 cup chopped green onions or chives
-salt (to taste)
-oil (to taste

Process
-Crumble and mash the feta with fork until no longer in clumps
-Add oil, 1 teaspoon at a time, and mix until smooth but still
thick
-Add salt to taste and mix
-Add green onions and mix trying to not smash green onions
-Serve with some crackers or bread
